

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.015

Varicose veins of right lower extremity with ulcer other part of foot

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.015  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Applicable To Varicose veins of right lower extremity with ulcer of toe

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of right foot 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot with bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot with fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot with muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot, skin breakdown 

ICD-10-CM  I83.015  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
