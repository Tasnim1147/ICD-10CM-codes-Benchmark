

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code O10.011

Pre-existing essential hypertension complicating pregnancy, first trimester

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 O10.011  is applicable to maternity patients aged 12 - 55 years inclusive. O10.011  is applicable to female patients. O10.011  is applicable to mothers in the first trimester of pregnancy, which is defined as less than 14 weeks since the first day of the last menstrual period. 

ICD-10-CM  O10.011  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 817  Other antepartum diagnoses with o.r. Procedures with mcc 
 818  Other antepartum diagnoses with o.r. Procedures with cc 
 819  Other antepartum diagnoses with o.r. Procedures without cc/mcc 
 831  Other antepartum diagnoses without o.r. Procedures with mcc 
 832  Other antepartum diagnoses without o.r. Procedures with cc 
 833  Other antepartum diagnoses without o.r. Procedures without cc/mcc
