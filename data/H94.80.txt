

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code H94.80

Other specified disorders of ear in diseases classified elsewhere, unspecified ear

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 H94.80  describes the manifestation of an underlying disease, not the disease itself. 

ICD-10-CM  H94.80  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 154  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses with mcc 
 155  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses with cc 
 156  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses without cc/mcc
