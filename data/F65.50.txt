

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code F65.50

Sadomasochism, unspecified

Approximate Synonyms

 Sadomasochism 

Clinical Information

 Derivation of pleasure from infliction of physical or mental pain on others and oneself, with presence of high degree of destructiveness.
