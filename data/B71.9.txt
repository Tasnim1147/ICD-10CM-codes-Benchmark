

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code B71.9

Cestode infection, unspecified

Applicable To Tapeworm (infection) NOS

Approximate Synonyms

 Cestode infection 

Clinical Information

 Infections with true tapeworms of the helminth subclass cestoda. 

ICD-10-CM  B71.9  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 371  Major gastrointestinal disorders and peritoneal infections with mcc 
 372  Major gastrointestinal disorders and peritoneal infections with cc 
 373  Major gastrointestinal disorders and peritoneal infections without cc/mcc
