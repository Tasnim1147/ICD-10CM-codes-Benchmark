

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.021

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with ulcer of thigh

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.021  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of left thigh 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh, skin breakdown 

ICD-10-CM  I83.021  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
