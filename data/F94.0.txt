Approximate Synonyms

 Elective mutism 
 Mutism, selective 

Clinical Information

 A persistent failure to speak in certain social situations (i.e., school) where speaking is expected, despite speaking in other situations. 

ICD-10-CM  F94.0  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 886  Behavioral and developmental disorders
