

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.92

Asymptomatic varicose veins of left lower extremity

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.92  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Acute aneurysm of left popliteal vein 
 Acute left popliteal vein aneurysm 
 Chronic aneurysm of left popliteal vein 
 Chronic left popliteal vein aneurysm 
 Varicose veins of left leg 
 Varicose veins of left lower limb 

ICD-10-CM  I83.92  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
