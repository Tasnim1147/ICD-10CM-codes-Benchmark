

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.813

Varicose veins of bilateral lower extremities with pain

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.813  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Pain co-occurrent and due to varicose veins of bilateral legs 
 Varicose veins of bilateral legs with pain 
 Varicose veins of both legs with pain 

ICD-10-CM  I83.813  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
