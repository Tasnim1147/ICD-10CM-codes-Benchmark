

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.221

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with both ulcer of thigh and inflammation

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.221  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation and bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation and fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation and muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation, fat layer exposed 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left thigh with inflammation, skin layer only 

ICD-10-CM  I83.221  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
