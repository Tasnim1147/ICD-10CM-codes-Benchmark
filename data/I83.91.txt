

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.91

Asymptomatic varicose veins of right lower extremity

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.91  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Acute aneurysm of right popliteal vein 
 Acute right popliteal vein aneurysm 
 Chronic aneurysm of right popliteal vein 
 Chronic right popliteal vein aneurysm 
 Varicose veins of right leg 
 Varicose veins of right lower limb 

ICD-10-CM  I83.91  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
