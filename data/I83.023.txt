

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.023

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with ulcer of ankle

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.023  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of left ankle 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle, skin breakdown 

ICD-10-CM  I83.023  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
