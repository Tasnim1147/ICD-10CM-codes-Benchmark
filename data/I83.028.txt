

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.028

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with ulcer other part of lower leg

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.028  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of left lower leg 
 Varicose ulcer of left lower leg limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of left lower leg with bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left lower leg with fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of left lower leg with muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left lower leg, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left lower leg, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left lower leg, skin breakdown 

ICD-10-CM  I83.028  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
