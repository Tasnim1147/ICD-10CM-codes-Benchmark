

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.025

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with ulcer other part of foot

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.025  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Applicable To Varicose veins of left lower extremity with ulcer of toe

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of left foot 
 Varicose ulcer of left foot limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of left foot with bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left foot with fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of left foot with muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left foot, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left foot, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left foot, skin breakdown 

ICD-10-CM  I83.025  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
