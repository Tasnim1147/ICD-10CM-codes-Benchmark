

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code D40.10

Neoplasm of uncertain behavior of unspecified testis

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 D40.10  is applicable to male patients. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Gonadoblastoma of testis 
 Gonadoblastoma, male 
 Leydig cell tumor of testis 
 Neoplasm of testis, leydig cell tumor 
 Neoplasm of uncertain behavior of testicular sex cord 
 Neoplasm of uncertain behavior of testis 
 Neoplasm of uncertain behavior of testis, sex cord

 715  Other male reproductive system o.r. Procedures for malignancy with cc/mcc 
 716  Other male reproductive system o.r. Procedures for malignancy without cc/mcc 
 722  Malignancy, male reproductive system with mcc 
 723  Malignancy, male reproductive system with cc 
 724  Malignancy, male reproductive system without cc/mcc
