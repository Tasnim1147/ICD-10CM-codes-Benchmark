

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.223

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with both ulcer of ankle and inflammation

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.223  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation and bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation and fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation and muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation, fat layer exposed 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left ankle with inflammation, skin layer only 

ICD-10-CM  I83.223  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc
