

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I88.1

Chronic lymphadenitis, except mesenteric

Applicable To Adenitis Lymphadenitis

Approximate Synonyms

 Chronic lymphadenitis 
 Lymphadenitis, chronic 

Clinical Information

 A disorder characterized by an infectious process involving the lymph nodes. 
 An acute or chronic infectious process affecting the lymph nodes. 
 Inflammation of the lymph nodes. 

ICD-10-CM  I88.1  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 814  Reticuloendothelial and immunity disorders with mcc 
 815  Reticuloendothelial and immunity disorders with cc 
 816  Reticuloendothelial and immunity disorders without cc/mcc
