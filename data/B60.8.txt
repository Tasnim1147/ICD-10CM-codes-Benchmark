

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code B60.8

Other specified protozoal diseases

Applicable To Microsporidiosis

Approximate Synonyms

 Bilateral microsporidial keratitis 
 Infection by microsporida 
 Infection by microsporidia 
 Keratitis, microsporidia 
 Left microsporidial keratitis 
 Microsporida infection 
 Microsporidia keratitis 
 Right microsporidial keratitis 

Clinical Information

 A protozoal parasitic infection caused by microsporidia. It occurs in immunocompromised patients, causing diarrhea and wasting. 
 Infections with fungi of the phylum microsporidia.
