

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code J92.0

Pleural plaque with presence of asbestos

Approximate Synonyms

 Asbestos-induced pleural plaque 
 Pleural plaque due to asbestos exposure 

ICD-10-CM  J92.0  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 193  Simple pneumonia and pleurisy with mcc 
 194  Simple pneumonia and pleurisy with cc 
 195  Simple pneumonia and pleurisy without cc/mcc
