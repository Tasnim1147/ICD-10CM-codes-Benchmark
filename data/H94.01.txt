

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code H94.01

Acoustic neuritis in infectious and parasitic diseases classified elsewhere, right ear

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 H94.01  describes the manifestation of an underlying disease, not the disease itself. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Right acoustic neuritis due to infectious disease 
 Right acoustic neuritis in infectious disease 

ICD-10-CM  H94.01  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 154  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses with mcc 
 155  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses with cc 
 156  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses without cc/mcc
