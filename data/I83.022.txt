

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.022

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with ulcer of calf

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.022  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of left calf 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf with bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf with fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf with muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf, skin breakdown 

ICD-10-CM  I83.022  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
