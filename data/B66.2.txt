

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code B66.2

Dicroceliasis

Applicable To Infection due to Dicrocoelium dendriticum Lancet fluke infection

Approximate Synonyms

 Dicrocoeliasis 

Clinical Information

 Infection with flukes of the genus dicrocoelium.
