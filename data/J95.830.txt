

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code J95.830

Postprocedural hemorrhage of a respiratory system organ or structure following a respiratory system procedure

Approximate Synonyms

 Bleeding as complication of tonsil surgery 
 Complication of tonsil surgery, with bleeding 
 Postprocedural pulmonary hemorrhage, following pulmonary procedure 
 Posttonsillectomy hemorrhage
