

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code G40.001

Localization-related (focal) (partial) idiopathic epilepsy and epileptic syndromes with seizures of localized onset, not intractable, with status epilepticus

Approximate Synonyms

 Epilepsy, partial idiopathic with status epilepticus 
 Idiopathic partial epilepsy with status epilepticus 

ICD-10-CM  G40.001  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 023  Craniotomy with major device implant or acute complex cns principal diagnosis with mcc or chemotherapy implant or epilepsy with neurostimulator 
 100  Seizures with mcc 
 101  Seizures without mcc
