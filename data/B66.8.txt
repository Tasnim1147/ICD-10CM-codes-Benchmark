

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code B66.8

Other specified fluke infections

Applicable To Echinostomiasis Heterophyiasis Metagonimiasis Nanophyetiasis Watsoniasis

Approximate Synonyms

 Echinostomiasis 

Clinical Information

 Infection by flukes of the genus echinostoma.
