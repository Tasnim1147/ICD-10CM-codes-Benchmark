

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.214

Varicose veins of right lower extremity with both ulcer of heel and midfoot and inflammation

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.214  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Applicable To Varicose veins of right lower extremity with both ulcer of plantar surface of midfoot and inflammation
