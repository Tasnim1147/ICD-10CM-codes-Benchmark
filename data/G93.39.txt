

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code G93.39

Other post infection and related fatigue syndromes

 G93.39  is a billable/specific ICD-10-CM code that can be used to indicate a diagnosis for reimbursement purposes. 
 ICD-10-CM  G93.39  is a  new 2023 ICD-10-CM code  that became effective on October 1, 2022. 
 This is the American ICD-10-CM version of  G93.39  - other international versions of ICD-10  G93.39  may differ. 

ICD-10-CM  G93.39  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 947  Signs and symptoms with mcc 
 948  Signs and symptoms without mcc
