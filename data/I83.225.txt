

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.225

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with both ulcer other part of foot and inflammation

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.225  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive.
