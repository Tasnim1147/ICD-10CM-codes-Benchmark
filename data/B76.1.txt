

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code B76.1

Necatoriasis

Applicable To Infection due to Necator americanus

Clinical Information

 Infection of humans or animals with hookworms of the genus necator. The resulting anemia from this condition is less severe than that from ancylostomiasis. 

ICD-10-CM  B76.1  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 371  Major gastrointestinal disorders and peritoneal infections with mcc 
 372  Major gastrointestinal disorders and peritoneal infections with cc 
 373  Major gastrointestinal disorders and peritoneal infections without cc/mcc
