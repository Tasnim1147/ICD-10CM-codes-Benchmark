

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code J96.20

Acute and chronic respiratory failure, unspecified whether with hypoxia or hypercapnia

Approximate Synonyms

 Acute and chronic posttraumatic respiratory failure 
 Acute-on-chronic respiratory failure 
 Respiratory failure, acute on chronic
