

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code H94.00

Acoustic neuritis in infectious and parasitic diseases classified elsewhere, unspecified ear

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 H94.00  describes the manifestation of an underlying disease, not the disease itself. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Acoustic neuritis due to infectious disease 
 Acoustic neuritis in infectious disease 

ICD-10-CM  H94.00  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 154  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses with mcc 
 155  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses with cc 
 156  Other ear, nose, mouth and throat diagnoses without cc/mcc
