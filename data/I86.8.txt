

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I86.8

Varicose veins of other specified sites

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I86.8  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Applicable To Varicose ulcer of nasal septum

Approximate Synonyms

 Acute venous aneurysm 
 Bleeding prostate varices 
 Bleeding varices of prostate 
 Chronic aneurysm of left internal jugular vein 
 Chronic aneurysm of right internal jugular vein 
 Chronic left internal jugular vein aneurysm 
 Chronic right internal jugular vein aneurysm 
 Varicose veins of nasal septum with ulcer 
 Varicose veins, nasal septum, with ulcer 
 Varicosity of left arm 
 Varicosity of left upper limb 
 Varicosity of right arm 
 Varicosity of right upper limb 

ICD-10-CM  I86.8  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
