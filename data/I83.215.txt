

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.215

Varicose veins of right lower extremity with both ulcer other part of foot and inflammation

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.215  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Applicable To Varicose veins of right lower extremity with both ulcer of toe and inflammation

 Varicose ulcer of right foot with inflammation, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot with inflammation, fat layer exposed 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot with inflammation, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right foot with inflammation, skin layer only
