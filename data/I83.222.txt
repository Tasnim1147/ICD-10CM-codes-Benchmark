

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.222

Varicose veins of left lower extremity with both ulcer of calf and inflammation

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.222  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive.

 Varicose ulcer of left calf with inflammation limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf with inflammation, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf with inflammation, fat layer exposed 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf with inflammation, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of left calf with inflammation, skin layer only
