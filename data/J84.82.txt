

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code J84.82

Adult pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 J84.82  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Applicable To Adult PLCH

ICD-10-CM  J84.82  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 196  Interstitial lung disease with mcc 
 197  Interstitial lung disease with cc 
 198  Interstitial lung disease without cc/mcc
