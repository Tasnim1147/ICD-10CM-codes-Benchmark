

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.212

Varicose veins of right lower extremity with both ulcer of calf and inflammation

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.212  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive.

 Varicose ulcer of right calf with inflammation limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of right calf with inflammation, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right calf with inflammation, fat layer exposed 
 Varicose ulcer of right calf with inflammation, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right calf with inflammation, skin layer only
