

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code B66.3

Fascioliasis

Applicable To Infection due to Fasciola gigantica Infection due to Fasciola hepatica Infection due to Fasciola indica Sheep liver fluke disease

Approximate Synonyms

 Fasciola hepatica infection 
 Liver fluke infection 
 Sheep liver fluke infection 

Clinical Information

 Liver disease caused by infections with parasitic flukes of the genus fasciola, such as fasciola hepatica. 

ICD-10-CM  B66.3  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 441  Disorders of liver except malignancy, cirrhosis or alcoholic hepatitis with mcc 
 442  Disorders of liver except malignancy, cirrhosis or alcoholic hepatitis with cc 
 443  Disorders of liver except malignancy, cirrhosis or alcoholic hepatitis without cc/mcc
