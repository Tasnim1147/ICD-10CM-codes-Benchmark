

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.211

Varicose veins of right lower extremity with both ulcer of thigh and inflammation

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.211  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

Approximate Synonyms

 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation 
 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation and bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation and fat layer exposure 
 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation and muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation limited to skin layer 
 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation, bone necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation, fat layer exposed 
 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation, muscle necrosis 
 Varicose ulcer of right thigh with inflammation, skin layer only 

ICD-10-CM  I83.211  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
