

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code I83.899

Varicose veins of unspecified lower extremity with other complications

ICD-10-CM Coding Rules

 I83.899  is applicable to adult patients aged 15 - 124 years inclusive. 

ICD-10-CM  I83.899  is grouped within Diagnostic Related Group(s) (MS-DRG  v 40.0):

 299  Peripheral vascular disorders with mcc 
 300  Peripheral vascular disorders with cc 
 301  Peripheral vascular disorders without cc/mcc
