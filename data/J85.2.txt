

2023 ICD-10-CM Diagnosis Code J85.2

Abscess of lung without pneumonia

Applicable To Abscess of lung NOS

Approximate Synonyms

 Abscess of lung 
 Lung abscess 

Clinical Information

 A bacterial, fungal or parasitic abscess that develops in the lung parenchyma. Causes include aspiration pneumonia, necrotizing pneumonia, necrotizing malignant tumors, and wegener's granulomatosis. 
 Solitary or multiple collections of pus within the lung parenchyma as a result of infection by bacteria, protozoa, or other agents.
